Cancellation Policy:
All Group Classes and Private / Semi-Private Sessions have a 24-hour cancellation/change policy. Late canceled reservations are subject to full charge.

Memberships:
30 days written notice is required to change or cancel a membership. Memberships may be suspended for up to 60 days cumulatively. Restrictions apply. Memberships may not be canceled during a hold.

Packages:
All packages have a 4-month expiration from the date of purchase unless otherwise noted. Class packages may not be shared and are not transferable.

All purchases are non-refundable.
