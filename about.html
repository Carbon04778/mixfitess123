Our Team

About Us

Meet the founder and instructors behind MIXX Fitness — a team dedicated to helping you move better, feel stronger, and reach your goals.

Nellie Brook

Founder of MIXX
PORTRAIT — NELLIE BROOK 480 × 560px

After earning her Master of Arts degree in Exercise and Sport Science from the University of Sydney, Nellie has spent more than 20 years developing and teaching Pilates programs for clients with specific medical needs, including chronic back pain, spinal stenosis, hip and knee replacements, and more.

Her experience includes six years as the Pilates Department Manager for Fitness and Wellness Services in New Jersey, as well as owning and operating her own Pilates studio in Sydney, Australia. Six years ago, Nellie opened MIXX Fitness in California, where she continues to bring her experience, care, and personalized approach to helping clients move better, feel stronger, and achieve their goals.

Smadar Sweiry

Instructor
PORTRAIT — SMADAR SWEIRY 480 × 560px

Smadar is coming to MIXX with 10 years of experience in teaching Pilates. She was first interested in Pilates because of her own back problem and was amazed to learn how Pilates improves injuries and posture. In 2011, she was licensed as a Pilates Mat Instructor and taught in Israel before moving to California in 2013. She is STOTT PILATES Certified, a mother of two, and enjoys staying active with Pilates, hiking, and yoga.

Jennifer Makower

Instructor
PORTRAIT — JENNIFER MAKOWER 480 × 560px

Jen stepped away from her career as a Speech-Language Pathologist working in special education in order to raise her three children. Now that her youngest is in his last year of high school, she is ready to start something new. She has always valued an exercise routine, but it wasn't until she discovered Pilates three years ago at MIXX studio that she truly enjoyed it. After watching her mom suffer at the end of life with several medical issues and physical limitations, she is committed to keeping active. Pilates is the perfect exercise to help with core strength, balance and flexibility which are all key for healthy aging. She looks forward to sharing her enthusiasm with students of all ages and abilities.

Irit Avidor

Instructor
PORTRAIT — IRIT AVIDOR 480 × 560px

Irit worked as a graphic designer and illustrator for over 12 years. Upon moving to the Bay Area, she felt it was time to make a change and add something new to her life. Having danced for 12 years, and doing Pilates regularly for more than a decade, she always considered making it more than just a workout. The right moment came when she discovered MIXX Fitness and its teacher training program. Irit loves the versatility of Pilates and its potential to help everyone, from healthy individuals to those with injuries. She moved to California two years ago with her husband, two kids, and their cat, Lulu. In addition to Pilates, Irit enjoys illustrating and designing, traveling with her family, exploring new places and enjoying good food.

Efrat Bigman

Instructor
PORTRAIT — EFRAT BIGMAN 480 × 560px

Efrat has been a Physical Therapist for over 25 years. During this time, she has worked in both hospital and home care settings with all age groups from children to the elderly, treating both chronic and acute conditions with physical activity, including Pilates. Efrat believes that physical activity is important at any age and in any condition. She loves hiking and trains everyday. She is a mother of three. With her youngest starting college, she now has the time to help you improve your strength, flexibility, and mobility. She looks forward to meeting you in her classes and sharing her experience.

A Note from Nellie

I created MIXX after years of exploring, studying and teaching different training and exercise methods.

As a mom of three and a wife to a super busy husband with my own career ambitions, I always tried to find the complete program that best fits my (changing) body and my (busy) mind, as well as my schedule, life priorities and most importantly… my budget. Workout by workout, through struggles and success, I created the MIXX concept.

I am so happy I now get to share MIXX with you! It's a revolutionary method, yet simple and focused on helping you reach your own goals for mind and body.

See you at the studio!

Nellie
